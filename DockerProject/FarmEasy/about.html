FarmEasy


ABOUT

*It is a well known fact that many of our farmers are poor. One of the many reasons for this is that they do not get the best price for their crop. And a reason for that is the presence of middlemen who take crops from farmers at low prices and sell them at high prices. Thus a farmer never gets a good price.
*In this view, a website could be designed that acts as a platform for the farmers to directly sell crops to consumers and thus get the best price. This is exactly what we decided to do. Our website, called FarmEasy, is a demonstration of this idea.

DESIGN OF WEBSITE

*Our website has an interactive homepage which serves as the main introductory and essential page which offers useful features.
*A Farmer registration form has been provided in the homepage where he enters his personal details as well as the crops which he wishes to sell.
*Besides this, the homepage also has several useful links for farmers.
*Useful photos of modern agriculture have been included.
*Videos of modern agriculture have also been included. These features help farmers to not only get themselves registered but also gain useful knowledge about agriculture.
Note: Further details of the homepage and the form have been included in the coming pages.
